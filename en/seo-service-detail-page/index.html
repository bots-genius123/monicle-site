What is Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) is the evolution of traditional SEO. While SEO focuses on ranking your website in a list of links, GEO focuses on making your brand the primary source of information for AI-driven platforms like Google AI Overviews (SGE), Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Gemini.

Instead of just optimizing for keywords, we optimize for context and citation. By structuring your data so that Large Language Models (LLMs) can easily synthesize it, we ensure that when a user asks a complex question, the AI “chooses” your brand as the authoritative expert to quote.
